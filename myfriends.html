For me friend is friendship, a state of lasting affection, self-respect, intimacy, and trust between two people. In all cultures, friendship is an important relationship throughout a person's life. I also had a good friend when I was in high school. My best friend in high school was Nur Aimi binti Tarmizi. She is a good friend who is able to listen to all my expressions. Our friendship still lasts until now and I hope it will last forever.

During my university days, I also had a few friends who became a place for me to lean on. Among them are Farah Farzana, Nur Hasnita and Nur Firhani. They are not only good to me, but they are also loyal listeners to every story I tell them.

We are always together. Do chores together, eat together and go out together. When at UiTM we always spent time together. Often Farah would bring the car and we would only be her passengers. When there is time we always go out to Amanjaya Mall to watch movies, Tanjung Dawai to buy salted fish and At Satay Semeling to eat. We usually go out on weekends to release stress. I feel very sad because our diploma will be over in two months. I feel like I don't want to part with them because I really love the friendship of the four of us. I think that's it and finally I want everyone to know that I love all my friends very much.
